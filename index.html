She came with us for our house journey, where we rented like 5 houses before settling in Jupiter, Fl. Even though my mom and my older brother were both allergic. It was because at the time my favorite animal was a cat and we wanted to try something different. His name was Gato. We were at the pet store one day, and my mom let be bring home a pink beta fish. I named her princess. We had princess for about a week until one day. You know what is not a good idea? Having a fish in a small, open tank that is very reachable if you can hop onto a counter. And you know what kind of animal can jump up on counters and loves to eat fish? Cats. Did we own one? Yes. I woke up one morning to find Princess just completley missing out of her tank. Our woking theory is that Gato ate her.
